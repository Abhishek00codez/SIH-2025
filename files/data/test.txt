### Math Questions

1. **What is the value of x in the equation 2x + 5 = 17?**  
   Answer: x = 6

2. **Calculate the area of a circle with radius 7 cm (use π ≈ 3.14).**  
   Answer: Approximately 153.86 cm²

3. **What is the square root of 144?**  
   Answer: 12

4. **Simplify the expression: 3(4 + 2) - 5.**  
   Answer: 13

5. **In a right triangle, if one leg is 3 units and the other is 4 units, what is the hypotenuse?**  
   Answer: 5 units

6. **What is 15% of 200?**  
   Answer: 30

7. **Solve for y: y/3 = 9.**  
   Answer: y = 27

8. **What is the perimeter of a rectangle with length 10 cm and width 6 cm?**  
   Answer: 32 cm

9. **Convert 0.75 to a fraction in simplest form.**  
   Answer: 3/4

10. **What is the next number in the sequence: 2, 4, 8, 16, ...?**  
    Answer: 32

11. **Calculate the volume of a cube with side length 5 cm.**  
    Answer: 125 cm³

12. **What is the value of 2³ + 3²?**  
    Answer: 17

13. **Find the median of the numbers: 5, 3, 8, 4, 7.**  
    Answer: 5

14. **What angle is formed by the hands of a clock at 3:00?**  
    Answer: 90 degrees

15. **Solve the inequality: x - 4 > 2.**  
    Answer: x > 6

### Science Questions

16. **What is the chemical symbol for water?**  
    Answer: H₂O

17. **Which planet is known as the Red Planet?**  
    Answer: Mars

18. **What is the process by which plants make their food?**  
    Answer: Photosynthesis

19. **What is the unit of force in the SI system?**  
    Answer: Newton (N)

20. **Name the three states of matter.**  
    Answer: Solid, liquid, gas

21. **What organ in the human body pumps blood?**  
    Answer: Heart

22. **What is the atomic number of carbon?**  
    Answer: 6

23. **What causes the seasons on Earth?**  
    Answer: The tilt of Earth's axis as it orbits the Sun

24. **What is the speed of light in a vacuum (approximately)?**  
    Answer: 3 × 10⁸ m/s

25. **Which gas do humans primarily exhale?**  
    Answer: Carbon dioxide (CO₂)

26. **What is the boiling point of water in Celsius?**  
    Answer: 100°C

27. **Name the largest bone in the human body.**  
    Answer: Femur

28. **What is the formula for kinetic energy?**  
    Answer: (1/2)mv²

29. **Which element has the symbol Na?**  
    Answer: Sodium

30. **What type of energy is stored in a battery?**  
    Answer: Chemical energy
